    About Us

    What We are and How we grow!


    GFive as the name itself avers, is a cohort of five consociates under one head. The patriarch of our endeavor, Mr. Rajesh Goyal, embarked on his voyage of success about two decades ago. He is a man having unrestrained desire to succeed and caters to you with all kinds of ethnic Indian attire. We have a colossal range of unstitched salwar suits and lehengas, sarees, kurtis, and ghagra chunnis in both designs as well as in materials. Our myriad collections make women feel more fragile and feminine. Our line of fashion wear is a visual delight for its mesmerizing embroidery, amalgamation of colors, and éclat materials. We primarily focus on the hand weavers or karigars. Our designs are an instant fashion, created by the hands of the weaver. It is a weaver's benchmark, a connoisseur's treasure evoking the image of the finest and authentic Indian women's wear. The enthralling flawlessness shown by our karigars has helped us to have our fiefdom in the industry. It has helped us to have a marvelous collection which is highly revered and preserved by our patrons. Our attire presents the sheer opulence, the majesty and zest, the glamour and grace and symbolizes true feminine splendor. We have all products suiting all the pockets without disparaging with quality, design, and customer satisfaction at any cost. We are contemplating having retail outlets in the city nearest to you. Today, we are a brand stigma. We hope to ensconce almost all the major cities across India with our retail outlets. We are also hoping to have a couple of outlets in some of the most famous cities across the world. We are looking forward to having business associates across India. We want you to have a long-term relation with our conglomeration and be our franchisee.
